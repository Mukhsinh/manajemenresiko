Formula
Reduction % = ((Inherent - Residual) / Inherent) × 100

Examples:
  • Inherent: 25, Residual: 9 → (25-9)/25 × 100 = 64.0%
  • Inherent: 15, Residual: 9 → (15-9)/15 × 100 = 40.0%
  • Inherent: 8, Residual: 4 → (8-4)/8 × 100 = 50.0%
